Project 01 Cash Controls

Reducing Cash Shortages Across 500+ Stores

The Problem

Monthly, non-standardized cash reconciliation across brands was producing financial losses and unreliable accounting. There was no consistent procedure, no daily visibility, and no mechanism to detect theft or non-compliance at the store level.

My Role

Co-designed standardized cash processing and accounting procedures from scratch. Built automated extraction, reconciliation, and variance-flagging pipelines. Implemented daily shortage reporting that gave regional leadership actionable visibility for the first time.

What Changed

Contributed to the design and implementation of standardized cash processing and accounting procedures as part of a three-person team, directly overseeing cash operations for approximately 500 locations. Built automated extraction, reconciliation, and variance-flagging pipelines that gave regional leadership daily visibility for the first time.

57%

reduction in cash shortages across all brands, sustained through automated daily reporting and standardized store procedures.

Project 02 Revenue Recovery

Building a New Credit Card Processing Cycle from Scratch

The Problem

There was no standardized procedure for credit card reconciliation, dispute management, or chargeback tracking. Recovery rate on disputes was effectively 0%. Fees, adjustments, refunds, and fraud-driven losses had no systematic detection or reporting.

My Role

Led end-to-end design of the chargeback tracking program. Architected SQL pipelines to ingest, cleanse, and warehouse transaction-level payment data. Built Power BI dashboards presenting recovery rates and dispute trends to executive leadership. Identified fraud cases (including tip manipulation, discount abuse, and gift card schemes) and built anomaly detection logic into the reconciliation pipeline. When a six-figure processor overbilling was identified across 130 Applebee's locations, the reconciliation infrastructure made it possible to quantify it precisely and support full recovery from our merchant.

What Changed

Dispute recovery rate improved from 0% to ~30%, enabling six-figure annual recoveries. Detailed visibility into fees and revenue-impacting factors now informs strategic decisions. Currently leading expansion of this infrastructure to all brands enterprise-wide.

0→30%

dispute recovery rate, built from scratch — supporting six-figure annual recoveries across a multi-brand portfolio.

Project 03 Forensic Analysis

Transaction-Level Analysis Supporting a $500K+ Legal Matter

The Problem

The corporate legal team needed to substantiate claims in a significant legal matter. The evidence lived inside millions of transaction records — unstructured, uncleaned, and spread across multiple source systems. No analyst had yet extracted or interpreted it.

My Role

Performed transaction-level data extraction, forensic analysis, and visualization of billing irregularities and fraud patterns across the relevant dataset. Translated raw transactional data into clear, structured findings that non-technical legal stakeholders could act on.

What Changed

Findings directly supported legal proceedings in a $500K+ matter. The work demonstrated that transaction-level analytical rigor, typically associated with audit or fraud teams, could be delivered from within the revenue operations function.

$500K+

legal matter supported through forensic transaction analysis — findings used directly by the corporate legal team.
